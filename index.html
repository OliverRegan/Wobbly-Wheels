Learn How to Roller Skate


Private and Group Classes in Graceville Brisbane


Book Now

Become a roller rink star, learn new skills every lesson, overcome your fear of falling and make new friends!


Whether you just got your first pair of skates, have dreams of gliding effortlessly around the rink, skated as a child and want to get back into it, or are looking for a fun, outdoor activity to enjoy with your children – Wobbly Wheels has a class for you! Our passionate roller skate coaches teach from 5 years old to 60+, proving that skating is fun, exciting and healthy for everyone!

You will learn how to skate safely, the fundamentals to becoming a confident skater, impressive new skills to show off at the rink, and conquer the fear of falling. Our lessons are tailored to the skill level of each individual ensuring that everyone is progressing; from simply skating forwards to spins, jumps and dance skating.

Our classes are hosted at the freshly paved Graceville Netball Courts where you enjoy an hour class that combines a variety of skate drills, skill progressions, fun games and music. If our class times don’t fit into your schedule or you don’t like being in a group class, we can provide private one-on-one classes and can travel to a location convenient for you.

Conquer the Skate Park


Love skating but want an exciting, new challenge? Join us for our skate park classes! Skate parks are no longer just for scooter kids and skateboarders as roller skating is quickly overtaking the skate park in popularity. Our coaches have over 3 years combined experience at the skate park and both find their true passion here.


You will safely learn to how to jump on and off boxes, drop in, pump and land safely. Become a master of ramps, bowls, half-pips, rails and stalls. Further skills will be added to the lessons to suit individual development and help our skater’s progress with the skills they desire to learn.

Book a Lesson

Beginner Court Lesson
$20

Ideal for children that have just started roller skating. They will learn the basic fundamentals of skating including rolling forwards, backwards, stopping, turning, jumping and falling safely. Lessons are fun and enjoyable through a series of games, musical enjoyment, friendship building and skill development.

Intermediate and Advanced Court Lesson
$20

Ideal for those that can skate forward confidently but want to advance their skills. You will learn to skate in many different variations including backwards, cross overs and zig zags. You will also learn a variety of turns, spins, jumps and basic dance skating moves. These classes include confidence building, games and thorough skill progressions.

Adult Court
$20

This class is suited to all adults and skills are tailored to all the individuals within the group. We offer a fun form of exercise, games and skill development. Let us know what you want to learn, and we will teach you!

Skate Park
$30

For those that are confident in skating forwards, backwards and have good balance. Learn the basics of dropping-in, skate park safety, and jumping. We will progress you to harder skills as you become more confident including stalls, cabbies, and carving. We also make sure to include tips for handling fear and anxiety. Confidence building is our strong suit!

The Importance of Wearing Protective Gear

We take safety very seriously as the occasional fall in inevitable. Accidents happen and it's best to be safe than sorry. Everyone attending must bring their own helmet and we strongly recommend wearing padding and guards. We do provide spare protective pads for those that need it but it is not guaranteed. If you're unsure where to buy the best gear to keep you safe, we can point you in the right direction.


Download and read through our waiver, it will need to be signed and acknowledged before your lesson. We look forward to seeing you there!
