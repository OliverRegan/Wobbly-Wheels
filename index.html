About Us

Welcome to Wobbly Wheels!

You will learn the fundamentals, make new friends, improve your confidence skating, and have fun in an outdoor environment with a variety of games and music. You will overcome your fear of falling with our coaches safety techniques; you will become a confident skater who is able to glide effortlessly, twril, dance and boogie your way into this exciting new hobby; you will also learn strong fundemental skate skills that will greatly benefit your ability to skate-park skate. We are based at the Graceville netball courts and are willing to travel for skate park lessons and private lessons. We are able to travel to the surrounding areas including Jindalee skate park, Goodna skate park and local netball courts. Book a class that suites you bellow!

Lessons
Beginner Netball Court Lessons
$20

First time skaters – in this class you will learn the fundamentals of skating including rolling forwards, stopping, and wobbling safely. Implemented through a series of games, musical enjoyment, friendship building and skill development.

Intermediate Netball Court Lessons
$20

Been skating for a while (should be able to confidently skate forwards before attending) – you will learn to skate in many different variations including backwards, cross overs, and zig zags. You will also learn turns, spins, coordination, and basic jumps. These classes include confidence building, games and thorough skill progressions.

Advanced Netball Court Lessons
$20

Confident skaters (should be able to turn, skate forwards and backwards) – you will lean to turn on one foot, some dance skate moves, advanced jumps, advanced coordination drills and skate park preparation.

Adult Netball Court
$20

This class is suited to all adults. We offer exercise, games, skill development and our lessons will be tailored to our roller skaters as a group and as individuals. Let us know what you want to learn, and we will teach you!

Skate Park Beginner
$30

Should already have completed the advance netball court lessons and have been approved to move into this class by a coach (feel free to enquire!). You will learn to jump on and off boxes, how to pump, how to land safely, and more! Also, the fundamentals of skate park skating and how to safely navigate a skate park.

Skate Park Advanced
$30

Should already have completed the beginner skate park lessons and have been approved to move into this class by a coach (feel free to enquire!). You will learn to use ramps, bowls, half-pipes, rails, and blocks. Including skills such as dropping-in down a ramp, carving a bowl and stalling. Further skills will be added to the lessons to suit individual development and help our skaters progress with the skills they desire to learn.

Adult Skate Park
$30

This class is suited to all adults. These lessons are tailored based on our roller skaters as a group and as individuals. You can learn the basics of dropping-in, skatepark safety, and jumping. We will progress you to harder skills as you become more confident including stalls, cabbies, and carving. We also make sure to include tips for handling fear and anxiety. Confidence building is our strong suit!

Private Lessons
$40 Netball Court
$45 Skatepark

We offer private lessons for both netball court skaters and skate park skaters. If you wish to attend a private skatepark lesson make sure to have attended our advanced netball court lessons and have a discussion with one of our coaches. If you wish to have a private lesson with your family or friends, there is a discount. Feel free to ask for a location recommendation, if you have a location you would like your private lesson to be held at we are happy to accommodate just enquire.
